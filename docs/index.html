Everything in one window

Built for the way you actually work with embeddings

Stop dropping into a Python REPL just to peek at a collection. ChromaMind gives RAG engineers a real interface for inspecting and managing vector data.

Browse every collection

Filter, favorite, and switch between collections instantly. Your whole database, organized in a sidebar you can navigate by keyboard.

Read full documents

Every row opens a detail panel with the complete document text.

Inspect embeddings on demand

Vectors stay out of your way until you need them. Expand any row to reveal its full embedding.

Filter by metadata

Slice collections by any metadata field. Find the exact records you're debugging without scrolling through thousands of rows.

Create & manage collections

Spin up new collections with metadata, or delete.

Local-first & fast

Connects straight to your ChromaDB instance — local or remote. Paginated, responsive, and built to stay snappy at scale.

Customize the look

Tweak accent colors, dark/light mode, and layout density from a built-in theme editor with live preview.

Zero setup

Works with whatever ChromaDB you're already running

Enter a host and port. That's it. ChromaMind connects over ChromaDB's HTTP API — no agent, no sidecar, nothing extra to install.

Local or remote. If ChromaDB is reachable from your machine, ChromaMind can connect to it.
Browsing doesn't touch your data. Writes and deletes require an explicit action — you won't accidentally modify anything.
Runs entirely on your machine. No telemetry, no cloud sync, no third-party requests. Just the app talking to your database.
